Welcome to Autocross Enthusiasts

Welcome to Autocross Enthusiasts, the ultimate online site for all things related to the world of autocross. Whether you are an experienced driver, a novice who is just starting, or simply a big fan of all things motorsport, our website is designed to provide you with valuable insights, tips, and resources to fuel your passion for autocross. Here, we want to build and grow a strong community for all car enthusiasts in southeastern Idaho where participants can share knowledge, stay updated on local events, and enhance their driving skills and experiences with the events.
