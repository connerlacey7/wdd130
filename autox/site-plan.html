Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to provide a comprehensive resource for autocross enthusiasts. This website will feature information about upcoming events, tips for improving driving skills, car modification advice, and profiles of notable autocross drivers and their vehicles.

Audience

My audience will be those who want to be involved with autoctross. It will help beginners get started and help veterans get better and learn more.

Content

Home page

Welcome to Autocross Enthusiasts, the ultimate online site for all things related to the world of autocross. Whether you are an experienced driver, a novice who is just starting, or simply a big fan of all things motorsport, our website is designed to provide you with valuable insights, tips, and resources to fuel your passion for autocross. Here, we want to build and grow a strong community for all car entusiasts in southeastern Idaho where participants can share knowledge, stay updated on local events, and enhance their driving skills and experiences with the events.

Images for the Home page

Events

Our event calendar provides a list of all the upcoming local autocross events, including dates, locations, registration information, and entry fees. Stay up-to-date with the latest events happening near you. Whether you are looking to compete or simply attend, our calendar is your go-to source for all autocross events.

Images for the Events

Tips and Guides

Through a series of articles and tutorials, these resources are great for getting advice on driving techniques, handling, and maneuvering to help drivers enhance their performance on the track. From beginners to advanced drivers, our resources are designed to help you get the most out of your autocross experience. Another part of autocross for some people is car modification. This covers everything from suspension adjustments to tire selection, ensuring that drivers can make informed decisions about improving their vehicles for the sport of autocross. Learn about the best upgrades and tweaks to enhance your vehicle's performance and get the competitive edge you need. We also offer a resource to find out what class you should be in for your car and modifications.
